Music is my ultimate escape and energy source outside the lab! I joined the WHU Symphony Orchestra as a section member in 2024, worked my way up to Principal Second Violinist in 2025, and had the absolute honor of being selected as the **Concertmaster** in 2026. Leading a 100-piece orchestra, tuning the ensemble before a grand concert, and collaborating with the conductor have been the most rewarding experiences of my college life—teaching me so much about leadership, deep listening, and teamwork.

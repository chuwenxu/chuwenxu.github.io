I love turning complex, messy real-world problems into elegant mathematical equations! My journey through mathematical modeling has been an absolute thriller. In the **2026 International MCM/ICM**, my team won the **Honorable Mention (Top 15-20% globally)** by building optimization and simulation models to design a space logistics network for lunar immigration. 

Beyond that, diving into the intensive **National Mathematical Contest in Modeling (CUMCM)** and the regional **"Central China Cup" (Huazhongbei)** pushed my coding and data-crunching skills to their limits. These 3-to-4-day hackathon-style sprints have been the ultimate test of resilience—teaching me how to stay calm under pressure, ideate under tight deadlines, and, most importantly, embrace the crazy fun of late-night brainstorming!

If playing the violin is about precision, singing in a choir is all about listening. As an alto section member in the "Haiyan" Youth Choir, I get to immerse myself in the beautiful world of multi-part harmony. Surviving our rigorous weekly vocal training and standing on stage for large-scale concerts has been an incredible journey. It’s taught me how to perfectly blend my voice with others to create something bigger than myself—the ultimate lesson in true collaboration.
